About Me

Hello! I'm Michael Bartolomeo.

I've been a Librarian and Technology Instructor since 2016, helping people of all ages build confidence with technology.

I design, teach, and speak about digital literacy programs on topics like artificial intelligence, online privacy, and essential tech skills. My goal is to make technology accessible and empowering for everyone.

Whether it's a hands-on workshop at a local library, a conference keynote, or a virtual webinar, I focus on meeting people where they are and giving them the tools they need to navigate the digital world with confidence.
